ইচ্ছে পূরণ(Icche Puron)

Welcome to ইচ্ছে পূরণ – A Personal Journey Through Strings and Soul
ইচ্ছে পূরণ is a space where my love for guitar meets the rich melodies of Hindi and Bengali music. This channel is all about sharing heartfelt guitar performances and easy-to-follow tutorials, created with care, precision, and a deep connection to the music I play.
Whether you're learning to strum your first chords or searching for a fresh take on a beloved song, you'll find content here that is approachable, musically rich, and thoughtfully made.
What You’ll Find on ইচ্ছে পূরণ:
🎸 Guitar Covers – A mix of contemporary hits, timeless classics, and personal favorites from the Hindi and Bengali music worlds, interpreted through expressive guitar work.
🎶 Step-by-Step Tutorials – Detailed, beginner-friendly lessons with chord breakdowns, strumming patterns, and practical tips to help you play songs smoothly and confidently.
🎧 A Personal Touch – Every arrangement and tutorial reflects my own interpretation and playing style, shaped by years of practice and a passion for making music more accessible.
Editor: Saumen C
All videos are edited with a focus on clarity and presentation, so learning is not only effective but also enjoyable.
This channel is my creative outlet—a way to explore music, express emotion, and hopefully make someone’s guitar journey a little bit easier and more meaningful.
